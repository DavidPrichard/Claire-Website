Research

I am interested in ergodic theory and number theory, particularly the connections to geometry. My advisor is Florin Boca.

Other Research Experience

University of Illinois

Illinois Geometry Lab, Fall 2014, Graduate mentor on a project with Jayadev Athreya , Francesco Cellarosi, and three undergraduate students, looking at the gap distribution and pair correlation properties of random subsets of sequences, such as the Farey seqence.

Project for Interdisciplinary and Industrial Internships at Illinois, Summer 2014, project with Zoi Rapti on creating a code to calculate the dispacement of nucleotides in a DNA tetrahedron. NSF grant DMS 1345032

Vanderbilt University

Senior thesis, School Year 2012-2013, looked at extending results of hyperbolic reflection groups of −3x20+ x2 1+ ... + x2n and −5x20+ x2 1+ ... + x2n to other quadratic forms

CalState Channel Islands REU

Summer 2012, Looked for a parameterization of the Pythagorean triples over the quaternions. Parameterized several classes of triples and obtained several methods of generating infinite sets of triples, NSF grant DMS-1005740
