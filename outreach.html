Outreach

In Spring 2016, I have a research assistantship to work on Illinois Geometry Lab outreach and planning for the Summer 2016 SIM/Mathways camp.

I was awarded a 2015-2016 Chancellor's Public Engagement Student Fellow by the University of Illinois Office of Public Engagement to start a summer program for local high school students. The Summer Illinois Mathematics Camp introduced students to proofs and applications of math to help them understand it is a far broader and more interesting topic than the formula memorization that is normally presented to them. By showing them some of the ways mathematicians approach problems, we hoped to encourage them to continue studying math beyond the high school level.

I am also involved with the AWM Sonia Math Day and Girls Engaged in Math and Science as well as other outreach opportunities in the community.
